timeline

  • April 2015 - July 2016

    CEO & Co-Founder @ Birdleaf

    Birdleaf was founded to help sales and marketing teams generate more revenue by turning email addresses into actionable insights.

    As co-founder and CEO I had a hands-on role in almost all areas of Birdleaf's development, from marketing to hiring, and had a particular focus on product management and investment. Shortly after founding the company we released a paid-only, B2B product, and quickly grew a strong list of industry-leading customers

  • April 2013 - March 2015

    CEO & Co-Founder @ CloudDock

    CloudDock developed a universal adaptor for cloud storage services, making them compatible. CloudDock enabled, for example, a Dropbox user to work seamlessly with a Google Drive user, without ever needing to have a Google Drive account.

  • May 2016 - present

    Self Employed IT Contractor

    I do freelance contract work, predominantly with IT startups. I focus primarily on product development, and work with companies on product planning, tech roadmaps, as well as product marketing and assistance in technical discussions with partners, customers or potential hires.
