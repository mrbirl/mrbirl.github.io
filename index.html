about

I'll keep this short :)

  • tech & startups

    I'm passionate about technology and growing businesses. I founded my first company, WestCoastSurfer, in 2010 while studying Computer Science & IT at NUIG. Since then, I've founded CloudDock and Birdleaf, and nothing motivates me more than the challenge of building a great product, and a great business. I also work with teams at other startups to advise and assist with product development, planning and strategy. I spend time on some side projects of my own, like an open source iPhone app for surfers - which introduces the section below nicely...

  • surfing

    I'm an avid surfer. Growing up in the West of Ireland I've been lucky enough to be be surfing most of my life. WindGuru and Magicseaweed are checked at least once a day to see when I might get out next. My first business, WestCoastSurfer, brought together my interest in technology, business and surfing, but now surfing is just for fun ;)

  • motorbikes

    When the surf is flat, my next favourite pastime is biking. Both my parents are bikers, and I started riding pillion at age 12. Now I riding myself, and I'm currently driving a '98 VFR800.

  • photography

    I take a lot of photos, and really enjoy amateur photography. I take almost all my photos on an iPhone 6 and GoPro Hero 4, and sometimes use an Olloclip. You can see a selection of my photos over on Instagram.

    Birdleaf was founded to help sales and marketing teams generate more revenue by turning email addresses into actionable insights.
